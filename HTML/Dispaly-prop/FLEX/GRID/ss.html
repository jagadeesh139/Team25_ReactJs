seasons

Seasons are the four divisions of the year based on changes in weather, ecology, and daylight hours: spring, summer, autumn, and winter. Is It Time to Redefine the Four Seasons of the Year ... Seasons are caused by Earth's tilted axis and its orbit around the sun. As Earth orbits the sun, different parts of the Earth receive the sun's direct rays at different times of the year.
